Dependency Annotations

To avoid problems with minification, we must annotate the service dependencies as strings in an array that includes the callback function.

Important (1): If the callback function is an anonymous function, the ending array bracket must be placed at the end of the function.

Important (2): Remember to annotate also the app.config() and anywhere else where you are injecting services.

Important (3): We can also use the $inject service: functionName.$inject = ['serviceName']
